# Audio-Responsive Presets Guide

The visualizer now has **boosted audio reactivity**. Different presets respond to different frequencies:

## Best for Low Frequencies (Bass):
- **Tunnel** - Tunnel expands with bass beats
- **Particles** - Particles move faster with bass
- **Retro Grid** - Grid lines pulse to bass

## Best for Mid Frequencies:
- **Waveform** - Waves respond to overall amplitude
- **Aurora** - Waves shift with mid-range tones
- **Plasma** - Flows change with mid frequencies

## Best for High Frequencies (Treble):
- **Fractals** - Zoom and color shift with treble
- **Glitch** - Glitch intensity increases with high frequencies
- **Starfield** - Star movement reacts to treble

## Most Overall Reactive:
- **Particles** - Responds to all frequencies
- **Kaleidoscope** - Colors and patterns react to all bands
- **Interference** - Wave patterns respond to all audio

## Testing Tips:
1. **Snap your fingers** to see treble response
2. **Hum or sing low** to see bass response
3. **Speak normally** to see mid-range response
4. **Clap** to see amplitude/beat response

Try different presets and watch for:
- Color changes
- Speed/intensity changes
- Pattern morphing
- Brightness changes

The audio is definitely being captured - look for visible changes when you make sound!
